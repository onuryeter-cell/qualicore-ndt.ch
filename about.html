About QUALI CORE NDT

QUALI CORE NDT was founded by Onur Yeter, a Materials Science Engineer and NDT Level 3 Expert based in Basel, Switzerland. With over 19 years of experience in highly regulated aerospace manufacturing and VIP aviation, Onur brings a proven zero-defect-tolerance mindset to every project.

The company was built on a simple principle: technical quality assurance should be precise, independent, and personally accountable. Drawing from extensive experience maintaining Nadcap and EN compliance—including achieving zero non-conformance findings during critical VIP aircraft inspections—QUALI CORE NDT offers direct access to senior-level expertise.

Whether you need hands-on inspection across six NDT methods, ISO 9001/14001 quality system auditing, or guidance on welding quality control from an International Welding Engineer (IWE), we deliver the rigorous documentation traceability required by aerospace, pharmaceutical, and heavy industry clients.

  • International Welding Engineer (IWE) – GSI-SLV
  • ISO 9001 & ISO 14001 Internal Auditor
